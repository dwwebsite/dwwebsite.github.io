Biography

I am an associate professor with tenure in the School of Information Sciences at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign (UIUC). I received my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science at UIUC. I am also affiliated with the Illinois Informatics , the Information Trust Institute, and the Center for Social and Behavioral Science (CSBS) at UIUC.

Research Interests

  • Social Sensing, Human-Centered AI

  • Big Data Analytics, Reliable Information Distillation Systems

  • Human-Cyber-Physical Systems, Smart Cities, Internet of Things/Everything (IoT/IoE)
