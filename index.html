Biography

I am an associate professor with tenure in the College of Engineering at the University of Notre Dame, with an appointment in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering. I received my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science at University of Illinois at Urbana Champaign (UIUC). I am also affilated with the Center for Network and Data Science, Notre Dame Environmental Change Initiative, and Center for Civic Innovation.

Research Interests

  • Social Sensing, Human-Centered AI

  • Big Data Analytics, Reliable Information Distillation Systems

  • Human-Cyber-Physical Systems, Smart Cities, Internet of Things/Everything (IoT/IoE)
