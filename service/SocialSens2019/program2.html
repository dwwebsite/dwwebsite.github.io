F: Full Paper (20 mins each)
EA: Extended Abstract (10 mins each)

8:30-8:45: Welcome by Chairs

 8:45-10:00 Keynote

Speaker: Dr. Kathleen M. Carley (Carnegie Mellon University)
Title: Social Cyber-Security Dynamics

Chair: Akhil Mathur (Nokia Bell Labs)

(F) Sofia Hurtado (Carnegie Mellon University), Poushali Ray (Carnegie Mellon University) and Radu Marculescu (Carnegie Mellon University), Bot Detection in Reddit Political Discussion

(EA) Nynke M. D. Niezink (Carnegie Mellon University), Offline Consequences of Echo Chambers

12:00-13:30 Lunch

13:30-14:50 Session 2: Human-in-the-Loop and Social-Environment Interaction

Chair: Rong Zheng (McMaster University)

(F) Anooshmita Das (University of Southern Denmark), Fisayo Caleb Sangogboye (University of Southern Denmark), Emil Stubbe Kolvig Raun (University of Southern Denmark), Mikkel Baun Kjaergaard (University of Southern Denmark), HeteroSense: An Occupancy Sensing Framework for Multi-ClassClassification for Activity Recognition and Trajectory Detection

(F) J.Fernandes (University of Coimbra), D.Raposo (University of Coimbra), S.Sinche (University of Coimbra), N.Armando (University of Coimbra), J.Sa Silva (University of Coimbra), A.Rodrigues (University of Coimbra), L.Macedo (University of Coimbra), H.Gonçalo Oliveira (University of Coimbra), F.Boavida (University of Coimbra), A Human-in-the-Loop Cyber-Physical Approach for Students Performance Assessment

(EA) Akhil Mathur (Nokia Bell Labs), Fahim Kawsar (Nokia Bell Labs), Nadia Berthouze (University College London), Nicholas D. Lane (University of Oxford), A Vision for Adaptive and Generalizable Audio-Sensing Systems

15:30-17:00 Session 3: IoT Issues & Discussions

Chair: Giovanni Beltrame (École Polytechnique de Montréal)

(EA) Jinsong Han (Zhejiang University), Feng Lin (Zhejiang University), Wenbo Shen (Zhejiang University), Kui Ren (Zhejiang University), Trustworthy Authentication for IoT with Human-and-Environment-in-the-Loop
